The Starry Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh

Year of Creation: 1889

The Starry Night is an oil-on-canvas painting by the Dutch Post-Impressionist painter Vincent van Gogh. It is considered one of the most recognizable paintings in Western art. The painting depicts the view from the east-facing window of his asylum room at Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, just before sunrise, with the addition of an idealized village.
