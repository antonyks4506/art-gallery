The Persistence of Memory

Artist: Salvador Dalí

Year of Creation: 1931

The Persistence of Memory is a 1931 painting by Spanish artist Salvador Dalí. It is one of the most recognizable works of Surrealism. The painting depicts a surreal landscape with melting pocket watches hanging from objects, suggesting the elasticity and unreliability of time in a dream state.
